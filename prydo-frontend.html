FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Prydo ID? +
A Prydo ID is a soulbound NFT on the Internet Computer blockchain. It's your unique, non-transferable on-chain identity that proves your membership in the Prydo community.
What is a soulbound NFT? +
A soulbound NFT cannot be transferred, sold, or moved to another wallet. It is permanently bound to the address that minted it — making it a true identity token.
How do I mint my Prydo ID? +
Click the "Mint Prydo ID" button, enter your wallet address, choose your avatar type, and submit. Your Genesis ID will be minted and recorded permanently on the ICP blockchain.
Is the Genesis ID really free? +
Yes! The Genesis tier is completely free for early community members. Only 100 Genesis IDs will ever be minted, so act fast.
What blockchain is Prydo built on? +
Prydo is built on the Internet Computer (ICP) — a next-generation blockchain that offers full decentralization, low cost, and high performance.
Can I have more than one Prydo ID? +
No. Each wallet address can only mint one Prydo ID. This enforces the "1 wallet, 1 identity" principle for a fair and authentic community.
